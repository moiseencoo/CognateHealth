Premier Provider of Occupational Health Solutions in Ireland.

We define occupational health as ‘a medical speciality, which focuses on the interaction of people’s health with their work. At Cognate Health, we partner with clients to design and deliver tailored occupational health solutions that enhance the physical, mental and social well-being of their workforce.

We have implemented an NSAI certified ISO 9001 compliant Quality Management System which is the industry standard in quality management systems. Our quality managed approach to occupational health solution delivery and a dedication to excellent customer service help to define the Cognate Health vision.

Our occupational health solutions are designed to reduce employer healthcare costs, increase productivity, reduce absenteeism, enhance employee morale, attract and retain high-quality employees.

We work with businesses of every size, helping them to become more productive, while lowering their overall healthcare costs.
