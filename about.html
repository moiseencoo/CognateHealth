Our History

It has become increasingly apparent that clients want more than just service provision. They want tailored solutions to their specific problems delivered in a timely, efficient and effective manner.
1993

Dr John Gallagher established Employment Health Advisors Limited (EHA) in 1993 and it grew to become one of the largest occupational health providers in Ireland.

1998

John led a joint venture with Mid West Occupational Health to create Corporate Health Ireland and EHA subsequently acquired Mid West Occupational Health, renaming the business ‘Employment Health Advisers Limerick’.

2015

In 2015, Dr John Gallagher and his team began the launch of Cognate Health.

2016

Development of the Cognate Health network
